BlockChain

What is the BlockChain

  • The blockchain is a data structure that allows for a secure record of ownership of digital assets
  • The blockchain is established within a distributed system of computers that house multiple ledgers of information
  • The blockchain allows for multiple ledgers to update their information in synchronization with its peers to maintain an accurate chronological record of all transactions
